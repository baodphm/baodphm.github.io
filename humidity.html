City Latitude vs Humidity (01/05/17)

Comparing latitude and humidy, being closer to the equator or having a high latitude both show higher instances of high humidity.

The scatter doesn't look like it has a line of best fit, with high humidty being the prevailing data found. The instances of low humidity are pretty evenly distributed across all latitudes. There is very little to show observable trends or correlation.
