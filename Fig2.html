Cloudiness

With the distribution of the scatter plot of city latitude vs cloudiness, there is not a clear pattern. Some observations could be made, but with a very low degree of confidence.

As the latitude increases, there appears to be more instances of increased cloudiness. Across all latitudes, 0% cloudiness is more frequent. The data, however, does not show any clear observable trends.
