City Latitude vs Max Temperature (01/05/17)

Surprising no one, the weather is significantly warmer at the equator. The southern hemisphere appears to have higher temperatures with the northern hemisphere experiencing lower temperatures.

With the negative correlation in temperature while looking at latitude, it can probably be assumed that the southern hemisphere is experiencing summer or late spring while the northern hemisphere is experiencing winter or fall.
